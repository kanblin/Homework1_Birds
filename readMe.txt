Homework1 - Birds

Capture and organize birds in an aviaries.  A larger Conservatory houses up to
20 aviaries with each aviary housing up to 5 birds.  These birds are captured and placed
by their tags.  Extinct birds are not able to be placed.  Bird food supplies
are tracked as birds of each tag are inserted.  Particular birds will have additonal
information displayed.
